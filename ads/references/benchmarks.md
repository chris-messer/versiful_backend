# Meta Ads Performance Benchmarks by Industry & Objective

Version: 1.0 | Last Updated: 2026-03-18

These benchmarks are derived from Meta's 2024-2026 data, industry reports (WordStream, AdEspresso, Hootsuite), and account audits. Use these to evaluate performance and set realistic targets.

---

## Industry-Specific Benchmarks

### E-commerce & Retail

| Metric | Benchmark | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| CTR | 1.0-1.5% | Higher for fashion/apparel |
| CPC | $0.50-$1.50 | Competitive category |
| CPM | $15-$40 | Q4 holiday season: $30-$60 |
| Conversion Rate | 2-4% | Product page to purchase |
| CPA (Purchase) | $20-$80 | Depends on AOV |
| ROAS Target | 3.0-4.0x | Blended (all campaigns) |
| ROAS - Prospecting | 2.0-2.5x | Cold audiences |
| ROAS - Retargeting | 6.0-10.0x | Warm audiences |
| AOV | $50-$150 | Average order value |
| Frequency (7d) | 2.0-3.5 | Prospecting |
| Frequency (7d) | 5.0-8.0 | Retargeting (higher OK) |

**Top Performers** (80th percentile):
- CTR: 2.0%+
- ROAS: 5.0x+ blended
- CPA: <30% of AOV

**Warning Signs**:
- ROAS <1.5x (losing money after COGS/fulfillment)
- CTR <0.5% (creative fatigue or poor audience fit)
- Frequency >5.0 (prospecting - creative burnout)

### SaaS & B2B

| Metric | Benchmark | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| CTR | 0.5-1.2% | Lower than B2C (longer consideration) |
| CPC | $1.50-$5.00 | Higher CPCs due to business targeting |
| CPM | $20-$50 | Business audiences more expensive |
| Conversion Rate (Landing Page) | 5-10% | Form fill or trial signup |
| CPA (Lead) | $30-$150 | Varies by deal size |
| CPA (Trial/Demo) | $50-$300 | High-intent actions |
| MQL to SQL Conversion | 20-30% | Marketing Qualified → Sales Qualified |
| Customer LTV | $500-$5,000+ | Justifies higher CPA |
| Lead-to-Customer Rate | 5-15% | Trial/demo to paid customer |

**Top Performers** (80th percentile):
- CTR: 1.5%+
- CPA (Lead): <$50
- Lead quality: >25% MQL to SQL

**Warning Signs**:
- CPA >$200 (unless LTV justifies it)
- Lead quality <10% MQL to SQL (targeting too broad)
- Trial-to-paid conversion <3% (product-market fit issue)

**SaaS-Specific Notes**:
- Free trial campaigns: Optimize for "Start Trial" event
- Freemium model: Optimize for "Complete Registration"
- High-ticket ($5k+ ACV): Focus on demo bookings, not self-serve trials
- Use CAPI to track trial → paid conversion (close attribution loop)

## Event Match Quality (EMQ) Benchmarks

| EMQ Score | Rating | Impact on Performance |
|-----------|--------|----------------------|
| **9.0-10.0** | Excellent | Best possible attribution, full optimization |
| **8.0-8.9** | Good | Strong performance, recommended minimum |
| **6.0-7.9** | Fair | Workable but suboptimal; improve data quality |
| **4.0-5.9** | Poor | Significant attribution loss; fix immediately |
| **<4.0** | Critical | Severe data loss; campaign performance severely limited |

**Target**: ≥8.0 for all conversion events

**How to Improve**:
- Add hashed email (`em`), phone (`ph`), first name (`fn`), last name (`ln`)
- Include `fbc` (Facebook click ID) and `fbp` (Facebook browser ID)
- Use CAPI to send server-side customer data
- Enable Advanced Matching in Pixel settings

## Creative Fatigue Indicators

| Metric | Healthy | Warning | Fatigued |
|--------|---------|---------|----------|
| **Frequency (7d) - Prospecting** | <2.5 | 2.5-4.0 | >4.0 |
| **Frequency (7d) - Retargeting** | <6.0 | 6.0-10.0 | >10.0 |
| **CTR Trend (14d)** | Stable/increasing | -10 to -20% | -20%+ |
| **CPM Trend (14d)** | Stable/decreasing | +10 to +20% | +20%+ |
| **CPA Trend (14d)** | Stable/decreasing | +10 to +25% | +25%+ |

**Action When Fatigued**:
- Pause creative immediately
- Introduce new creative (different hook, format, or angle)
- Expand audience (if too narrow)

## Learning Phase Benchmarks

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Conversions Needed** | 50 per week | Per ad set to exit learning |
| **Time to Exit Learning** | 3-7 days | With sufficient budget |
| **Learning Limited %** | <30% | % of ad sets stuck in learning |

**Budget Required to Exit Learning**:
- Formula: `(CPA × 50 conversions) / 7 days = Minimum Daily Budget`
- Example: CPA $10 → Need $71.40/day minimum
- Example: CPA $20 → Need $142.85/day minimum

## Budget Benchmarks by Business Size

### Micro-Budget ($500-$2,000/month)

- **Per Ad Set**: $15-$30/day
- **Total Campaigns**: 1-2 active
- **Strategy**: Single prospecting campaign + retargeting
- **Limitations**: May not exit learning phase; focus on retargeting for efficiency

### Small Budget ($2,000-$10,000/month)

- **Per Ad Set**: $30-$100/day
- **Total Campaigns**: 2-4 active
- **Strategy**: Prospecting (broad) + retargeting + testing
- **Learning Phase**: Should exit on most ad sets

### Medium Budget ($10,000-$50,000/month)

- **Per Ad Set**: $100-$500/day
- **Total Campaigns**: 3-6 active
- **Strategy**: Full funnel (prospecting, retargeting, retention) + creative testing + Advantage+ campaigns
- **Scaling**: Can test new audiences and scale winners quickly

### Large Budget ($50,000+/month)

- **Per Ad Set**: $500-$5,000/day
- **Total Campaigns**: 5-10 active
- **Strategy**: Advanced segmentation, Conversion Lift studies, multi-country expansion
- **Optimization**: Bid strategies, ROAS goals, sophisticated attribution

## Seasonal Benchmarks (CPM Fluctuations)

| Period | CPM Multiplier | Notes |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| **January** | 0.8x | Post-holiday drop (cheapest month) |
| **February-March** | 1.0x | Baseline |
| **April-June** | 1.1x | Slight increase (Q2) |
| **July-August** | 0.9x | Summer slowdown (B2B especially) |
| **September-October** | 1.2x | Back-to-school, Q4 ramp-up |
| **November (Black Friday)** | 1.8-2.5x | Highest CPMs of year |
| **December (Holiday)** | 1.5-2.0x | Competitive but high intent |

**Strategy**: Scale in low-CPM months (Jan-Feb), expect higher costs in Q4.

## Attribution Window Impact

| Attribution Window | Impact on Reported Conversions | Use Case |
|-------------------|-------------------------------|----------|
| **1-day click** | Lowest (50-70% of 7-day) | Conservative reporting, short sales cycle |
| **7-day click, 1-day view** | Standard (100% baseline) | Default, most common |
| **7-day click, 7-day view** | Higher (120-140% of standard) | Longer consideration, brand awareness |
| **28-day click, 28-day view** | Highest (150-200% of standard) | High-ticket, long sales cycle (B2B) |

**Recommendation**: Use 7-day click, 1-day view (standard) unless sales cycle >7 days.
