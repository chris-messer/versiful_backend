# Versiful Outreach Messaging Guidelines

## Core Principles

### 1. Always Identify the Sender
Recipients likely don't have our number saved in their contacts.

✅ **DO**: "Hi [Name], it's Versiful!"
❌ **DON'T**: "Hi [Name]!" (without identifying who we are)

### 2. Acknowledge Time Gaps Appropriately
For users who last contacted us weeks or months ago:

✅ **DO**: "It's been a while since we last connected"
❌ **DON'T**: "Thanks for reaching out" (implies recent contact)

### 3. De-emphasize Conversation History
Don't lead with features users may not value:

✅ **DO**: Focus on personalized guidance and Bible version choice
❌ **DON'T**: "Save your conversation history" as a primary benefit

### 4. Personalize When Possible
Use first names when available, but have fallback messaging:

✅ **DO**: "Hi Greg, it's Versiful!"
✅ **ALSO OK**: "Hi! It's Versiful." (when no name)

### 5. End with an Invitation
Give users a clear next step:

✅ **DO**: "What can we help you with today?"
✅ **DO**: "Reply anytime with questions!"
✅ **DO**: "What's on your heart today?"

## Message Structure

### Template Format
```
[Greeting] [Name], it's Versiful! [Acknowledgment of past interaction].

[Value proposition / Registration pitch]

[Call to action / Question]
```

### Length Guidelines
- Keep total message under 320 characters when possible (2 SMS segments)
- Shorter messages have better engagement rates
- Balance brevity with warmth and value proposition

## Engagement-Based Messaging

### High Engagement (3+ user messages)
These users have shown genuine interest. Emphasize that we noticed their engagement:

```
Hi [Name]! It's Versiful. We noticed you've been asking questions but haven't registered yet.

Create a free account at https://versiful.io to:
• Get personalized biblical guidance
• Choose your preferred Bible version

What's on your heart today?
```

**Key points:**
- Acknowledge they've been active ("asking questions")
- Emphasize personalization ("your preferred Bible version")
- Warm, engaging tone

### Medium Engagement (2 user messages)
Users who tried the service and came back. Light touch:

```
Hi [Name], it's Versiful! It's been a while since we last connected. We're here to provide biblical guidance anytime you need it.

Register at https://versiful.io for free to get personalized responses.

What can we help you with today?
```

**Key points:**
- Acknowledge time gap
- Remind of availability
- Simple value prop
- Open invitation

### Low Engagement (1 user message)
Users who reached out once. Very light touch:

```
Hi! It's Versiful. It's been a while since we last connected. We're here to provide biblical guidance whenever you need it.

Create a free account at https://versiful.io to:
• Personalized biblical guidance
• Your preferred Bible version

Reply anytime with questions!
```

**Key points:**
- Generic greeting (may not have name)
- Acknowledge time gap
- Low-pressure invitation
- Focus on availability

## What to Avoid

### ❌ Don't Be Presumptuous
- "Thanks for reaching out" when they reached out weeks ago
- "We're excited to help you!" (too salesy)

### ❌ Don't Oversell Features
- Long lists of bullet points
- Technical features users don't understand
- "Save your conversation history" as primary benefit

### ❌ Don't Be Pushy
- Multiple exclamation points
- Urgent language ("Don't miss out!")
- Pressure to register immediately

### ❌ Don't Send Without Context
- Never send without identifying as Versiful
- Never send without acknowledging past interaction
- Never send generic mass-message copy

## Exclusion Criteria

Never send outreach to:

1. **Users with skipMarketing=true** - They've explicitly opted out
2. **Toll-free numbers** - Can cause SMS loops
3. **Same-day signups** - Too soon, let them try the service first
4. **Spam/test accounts** - Identified by content like "Buh", "test", hostile questions
5. **Recent contacts** - If they texted in the last 7 days, they're still engaged

## Timing Considerations

### Optimal Send Windows
- Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM - 6 PM local time (if known)
- Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons
- Never send late evening or early morning

### Frequency Caps
- Never send to the same number more than once per 30 days
- If user replies to outreach, don't send another outreach for 90 days
- If user registers after outreach, mark as success and don't re-contact

## Tracking and Metrics

### Success Metrics
- Reply rate (target: >15%)
- Registration conversion (target: >5%)
- Unsubscribe/STOP rate (target: <2%)

### Red Flags
- Multiple STOP responses → Review messaging
- No replies after 20+ sends → Pause campaign type
- High carrier filter rate → Check content for spam triggers

## A/B Testing Guidelines

When testing message variations:

1. **Change one variable at a time**
   - Test greeting styles
   - Test value propositions
   - Test CTAs

2. **Minimum sample size: 50 per variation**

3. **Track:**
   - Reply rate
   - Registration rate
   - Time to reply
   - Sentiment of replies

## Compliance

### Required Elements
- Company identification (Versiful)
- Opt-out method (reply STOP)
- No misleading claims

### Prohibited Content
- Health/medical claims
- Guarantees of spiritual outcomes
- Emotional manipulation
- Excessive urgency or scarcity
